ME 405 Romi Pin Connections

Drive Left:
    Motor_Dir:  PB5 (blue)
    Motor_Eff:  PB4 (green)
    Enc_Ch1:    PA5 (yellow)        
    Enc_Ch2:    PB3 (blue)
    
    
Drive Right: 
    Motor_Dir:  PA1 (blue)
    Motor_Eff:  PA0 (green)
    Enc_Ch1:    PB6 (yellow)
    Enc_Ch2:    PB7 (blue)
    
I2C Ch 1 for BNO:
    SCL:        PB8 (blue)
    SDA:        PB9 (yellow)    
    
Line Sensors:
    Axial:
    L2:         PC2 (blue)      # Far left
    L1:         PC3 (green)     # Mid left
    C:          PA4 (gray)      # Center, Arduino pin A2
    R1:         PB0 (orange)    # Mid right, Arduino pin A3
    R2:         PC1 (purple)    # Far right, Arduino pin A4
    
    Front:
    FL2:        PA6 (blue)      # Far left         
    FL1:        PA7 (green)     # Mid left
    FC:         PC4 (white)     # Center
    FR1:        PA3 (orange)    # Mid right 
    FR2:        PA2 (purple)    # Far right
    
Lidar:
    Lidar:      PC0 (white)
